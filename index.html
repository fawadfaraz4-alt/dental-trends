Treatments & services

Comprehensive dental care for adults and children – with a focus on long‑term results and comfort.

All major dental procedures under one roof
IM
Dental implants
Single and multiple implants to replace missing teeth with a natural look and chewing strength, carefully planned using X‑rays.
CR
Crowns & bridges
Metal‑ceramic and all‑ceramic caps to strengthen weak teeth, plus bridges to close gaps without removable dentures.
DN
Dentures (complete & partial)
Removable dentures for patients missing several or all teeth, designed for comfort and stable fit.
RC
Root canal & fillings
Pain‑controlled root canal treatments followed by tooth‑coloured composite fillings for a natural look.
SM
Smile makeovers & veneers
Veneers, laminates, gum depigmentation and reshaping to correct gaps, stains and uneven teeth.
BR
Braces & orthodontics
Braces and alignment plans to correct crowding, spacing and bite issues in teens and adults.
KD
Paediatric (kids’) dentistry
Gentle treatment for children with patient explanation and small gifts to reduce fear of the dentist.
SC
Scaling, polishing & hygiene
Deep cleaning to remove plaque and calculus, polishing and personalised brushing / flossing guidance.
XR
Digital X‑rays & diagnosis
Low‑radiation digital X‑rays and oral examination, including screening for systemic conditions that show in the mouth.
FM
Full‑mouth rehabilitation
Comprehensive planning for multiple missing or worn teeth, combining implants, crowns and bite correction.
